## Welcome

We're excited for you to join our troop! There are a small number of steps to complete, but you're welcome to attend meetings ahead of completing these tasks.

1. Register online with BSA ([Boys](https://my.scouting.org/VES/OnlineReg/1.0.0/?tu=UF-MB-492taa0150) / [Girls](https://my.scouting.org/VES/OnlineReg/1.0.0/?tu=UF-MB-492taa5150))
1. Submit [health forms](https://filestore.scouting.org/filestore/HealthSafety/pdf/680-001_ABC.pdf) to a troop adult leader
    * Each scout must complete and submit a paper copy of BSA Health Forms A and B1/B2 to attend any outdoor activities.
    * Attending any scouting trip over three days in length (e.g. summer camp) also requires BSA Health Form C.
    * **Include a printed copy of your insurance card when submitting health forms.**
    * Parents must also submit health forms ahead of attending scouting trips.
    * Health forms can be submitted at any weekly meeting.
1. Get a Troop 150 Uniform
    * The troop can help you get the correct uniform articles. Please check in with us before buying anything listed below. We have a collection of donated and well-maintained items available for re-use.
    * Uniform articles:
        * BSA official articles
            * Class A short-sleeve shirt ([boys](https://www.scoutshop.org/scouts-bsa-boys-uniform-short-sleeve-shirt-khaki-xs-xl.html) / [girls](https://www.scoutshop.org/scouts-bsa-girls-unfiorm-short-sleeve-shirt-khaki.html))
            * Class A shorts ([boys](https://www.scoutshop.org/scouts-bsa-boy-s-uniform-short-olive-8-20.html) / [girls](https://www.scoutshop.org/scouts-bsa-girls-uniform-short-648809.html))
            * [Belt](https://www.scoutshop.org/boy-scout-web-belt-606884.html)
            * [Green epaulets](https://www.scoutshop.org/boy-scout-shoulder-loops-64017.html)
            * Socks of any style (e.g. [crew style](https://www.scoutshop.org/boy-scout-uniform-coolmax-crew-socks-610336.html)), 2-3 pair are recommended
        * Troop provided (do not purchase from the Scout Shop)
            * Hat
            * Troop numbers
            * Troop class B t-shirt
            * Neckerchief slide
